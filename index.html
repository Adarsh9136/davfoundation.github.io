Why DAV?

Why DAV?

The key to enabling a future where autonomous vehicles, service providers, and users work together is communication.

In this future, an autonomous vehicle (e.g. a driverless car or drone), can discover, communicate, and transact with users (e.g. passengers or packages), service providers (e.g. charging stations, insurers), and even other autonomous vehicles (e.g. last mile delivery, long-distance transportation).

This future can be controlled by a few large corporations that control the entire supply chain, or it can be decentralized (like the Internet itself) allowing autonomous vehicles, services, and users to discover each other and transact with each other without the need for a central authority to inflate costs, or control and manipulate the market.

DAV is working to build this future, using the power of the blockchain.

How DAV works?

DAV is a framework of open-source software, open-source hardware, and a free decentralized marketplace where anyone can buy and sell autonomous transportation services using the DAV token.

This framework includes:

  • A P2P discovery protocol for autonomous agents to discover each other in a decentralized way
  • A P2P communication protocol that enables simple and efficient communication and mission planning between autonomous agents
  • Smart contracts for secure, and trustless transactions between autonomous agents
  • A smart token to enable secure transactions and payments between autonomous agents
  • A reputation system to track, identify, and flag bad actors
  • Software development kits to allow easy integration between existing AVs or other autonomous components and the DAV Network
  • Hardware components available off-the-shelf, or as open-source specifications, to connect autonomous agents to the DAV Network
  • Development and simulation tools to allow for easy development without an upfront hardware investment

Why Use Cryptocurrency and Blockchain?

The DAV token is a unique cryptocurrency that is used by drones, robots,
cars, and other vehicles that provide autonomous transportation services.
Why is a token needed for autonomous transportation?

  • A token enables a completely decentralized system and isn't controlled by any government, bank, or company
  • A token enables a global payment standard between vehicles and their supporting infrastructure
  • A token's built-in smart mission contracts enable trustless cooperation between multiple vehicles and service providers
  • A token enables its investors and early adopters to benefit from the growth of its economy over time while still being a completely free and open-source platform
